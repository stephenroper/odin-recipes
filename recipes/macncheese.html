Macaroni and Cheese

Description

  1. Bring pot of water to a boil on stove
  2. Add noodles. Stir and let boil for 8-10 minutes
  3. Strain noodles. Leave in strainer.
  4. Set stove temperature to low
  5. Add 1 tbsp vegan butter and 1/2 cup plant milk to pot
  6. Add cheese mix powder
  7. Whisk until saucy
  8. Add noodles back to pot
  9. Stir until delicious
  10. Devour
